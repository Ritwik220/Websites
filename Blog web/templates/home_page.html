Introduction

Hello. This is a website where you can write and store your blogs, you can also search other people and read their blogs if they made them public. You will have a choice of keeping the blog private or public. People other than you will be able to read your blog only if you made them public otherwise they can be read only by you. Warning: Even when the blog is set to private it is adviced to not write any unnecessary private information.
